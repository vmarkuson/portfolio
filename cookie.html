What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are used to remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience. Cookies can also be used for advertising purposes.

Types of cookies we use

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies - These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly.
  • Analytics cookies - These cookies allow us to track how users interact with our website and help us improve our services.
  • Advertising cookies - These cookies are used to deliver personalized ads based on your browsing history and preferences.

Managing cookies

You can manage cookies by adjusting your browser settings. You can choose to block cookies, delete cookies, or set your browser to alert you when cookies are being sent. However, please note that some features of our website may not function properly if you disable cookies.
